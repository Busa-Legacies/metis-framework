# Distribution

Primary responsibility: design and continuously refine Navore's local distribution model.

## Focus Areas
- Delivery zones and pickup location selection (customer density, travel time, farm routing, community access)
- Aggregation hub strategy
- Delivery and pickup process reliability and low operational cost
- Last-mile strategy development
- Customer feedback analysis → distribution improvements
- Alignment with Producer Specialists on farm capacity and demand

## Structure
- `zones/` — delivery zone maps, pickup location analysis, hub placement
- `last-mile/` — last-mile strategy docs and pilots
