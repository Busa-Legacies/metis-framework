# Strategy

**OKRs:** KR2.1 Company Roadmap · KR2.2 Systemic Bottlenecks · KR2.3 Dashboard · KR3.1 Revenue Initiative

## KR2.1 — Company Roadmap
Target: 12–18 month roadmap by quarter, with dependencies (tech, logistics, partnerships, grants) and measurable milestones. Format: what, where, when, why, who, how.

→ `roadmap/`

## KR2.2 — Systemic Bottlenecks
Target: Identify top company-wide bottlenecks (technical, operational, financial) and complete mitigation plans.

→ `bottlenecks/`

## KR2.3 — Dashboard
Target: Launch company dashboard tracking 3–5 core metrics. Scope requirements → dev team for time estimate.

Core metrics: order volume, churn, fulfillment reliability, spend

→ `dashboard-spec/`

## KR3.1 — Revenue Initiative
Target: 1 partnership or operational initiative that increases throughput, reduces costs, or unlocks institutional markets. Must include tied measurements.

→ Document initiative in `roadmap/` with KR3.1 tag.
